Who We Are

TimoTECH Films is a professional photography and videography studio dedicated to telling powerful stories through the lens. We specialize in weddings, music videos, commercial shoots, documentaries, and creative social media content.

Location

We are located in THARAKA NITHI County, MAARA SUBCOUNTY, MWIMBI WARD, KAARE Market, Kenya, with easy access to nearby regions for on-site shoots and productions. Our mobile team can travel to any location to meet our clients’ creative needs.

Our Profile

Since our founding, TimoTECH Films has worked with a variety of clients—individuals, brands, and organizations—to produce quality visuals that inspire and captivate. Our mission is to merge creativity with technology to bring your vision to life.


We pride ourselves on precision editing, cinematic techniques, and unmatched customer service that keeps our clients coming back.

Meet the Founder

The studio was founded by Timothy Mugambi, a passionate photographer and videographer with over 15 years of experience in the industry. Timothy’s dedication to his craft and his eye for detail have made TimoTECH Films a trusted name in visual storytelling.

Support Our Vision

TimoTECH Films is driven by a passion to produce higher-quality, cinematic content that inspires creativity and tells powerful stories. Our goal is to keep upgrading our equipment and skills so that we can deliver world-class photography and videography services.
